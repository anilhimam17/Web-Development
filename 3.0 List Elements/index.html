Anil's Recipe for the Best Sambar

Ingredients:

  1. Toor Dal
  2. Tomatoes
  3. Onions
  4. Okhra
  5. Drum Stick
  6. Raddish
  7. Green Chilli
  8. Lemons
  9. Sambar Powder
  10. Red Chilli Powder
  11. Salt

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and chop all the vegetables except Lemons
  2. Rinse and place the Toor Dal in a Pressure Cooker
  3. Place all the chopped vegetables in the Pressure Cooker
  4. Cook until the pressure cooker leaves two whistles blows
  5. After removing all the pressure from the Pressure Cooker, seperate all the Drum Sticks and Okhra
  6. Squash all the ingredients in the Pressure Cooker until the mixture is fine and has the consistency of a liquid
  7. Reheat the mixture with the Drum Sticks in a Wok or Stock Pot
  8. Add sambar powder and lemon juice to balance the taste of the stew
  9. Add water to manage the consistency of the vegetable stew
  10. Cook until the oil seperates from the water


There you go Savour yourself to some mouth watering Sambar
